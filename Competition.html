How many Trees can you plant,

in 5 minutes!

For the inauguration of our brand new website, we are launching a tree planting competition on the 15th of November. The rules are very simple:

  1. Each contestant has 5minutes to plant has many trees has possible.
  2. If a tree is not planted correctly, it won't be counted.
  3. Once the bell rings after 5 minutes, the contestant must stop.

If you are unsure on how to plant a tree correctly, you can refer to our FAQ. There are no entry fees, and their will be hot drinks and prizes for everyone. The winner of the compettion will earn a special prize!

Make sure to fill the form on the right side before the 15th of November if you are interested!
